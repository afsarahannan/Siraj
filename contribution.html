Contribution to History

  • 1756: Became the Nawab of Bengal after the death of his grandfather, Alivardi Khan.
  • 1756: Launched the Siege of Calcutta, capturing the British-held city.
  • 1757: Fought in the Battle of Plassey against the British East India Company.
  • 1757: Betrayed by his general, Mir Jafar, leading to his defeat and capture.
